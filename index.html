Qingzhong Ai

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), under the supervisor of Prof. Zenglin Xu. My research interests include deep generative models, approximation inference, representation learning and adversarial attacks.

Publications
Generative Oversampling for Imbalanced Data via Majority-Guided VAE
Qingzhong Ai, Pengyun Wang, Lirong He, Liangjian Wen, Lujia Pan, Zenglin Xu
AISTATS, 2023  
[PDF] [Code]

We propose a novel over-sampling model, called Majority-Guided VAE (MGVAE), which generates new minority samples under the guidance of a majority-based prior.

Edge Enhancement Improves Adversarial Robustness in Image Classification
Lirong He, Qingzhong Ai, Yuqing Lei, Lili Pan, Yazhou Ren, Zenglin Xu
Neurocomputing, 2023  
[PDF] [Code]

We propose a new method based on edge detection, named Edge-Enhancement (EE), which can explicitly make up for the missing shape information in frequency constraints and further enhance the adversarial robustness.

Stein Variational Gradient Descent with Multiple Kernels
Qingzhong Ai, Shiyu Liu, Lirong He, Zenglin Xu
Cognitive Computation, 2022  
[PDF] [Code]

We first extend Kernelized Stein Discrepancy (KSD) to its multiple kernels view called Multiple Kernelized Stein Discrepancy (MKSD) and then leverage MKSD to construct a general algorithm Multiple Kernel SVGD (MK-SVGD).

A One-Shot Reparameterization Method for Reducing the Loss of Tile Pruning on DNNs
Yanchen Li, Qingzhong Ai, Fumihiko Ino
IJCNN, 2022   (Oral)
[PDF] [Code]

We propose a one-shot reparameterization method, called TileTrans, to reduce the loss of tile pruning.

ByPE-VAE: Bayesian Pseudocoresets Exemplar VAE
Qingzhong Ai, Lirong He, Shiyu Liu, Zenglin Xu
NeurIPS, 2021  
[PDF] [Code]

We propose Bayesian Pseudocoresets Exemplar VAE (ByPE-VAE), a new variant of VAE with a prior based on Bayesian pseudocoreset.
